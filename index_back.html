I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Centro de Modelamiento Matemático (CMM), Universidad de Chile, working under the supervision of Jaime Ortega. My research focuses on numerical analysis, scientific computing, manifold learning, harmonic analysis, geometric methods, and data-driven algorithms for high-dimensional problems.

About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from IMPA in December 2021. Prior to that, I obtained my M.S. in Mathematics from IMPA and my B.S. in Mathematics from Universidad Industrial de Santander (UIS).

My work combines ideas from numerical analysis, harmonic analysis, geometry, inverse problems, manifold learning, and scientific computing.
